The Problem
People feel unsafe traveling after the pandemic. Typical outdoor planning apps don’t offer comprehensive safety information and an all in one booking experience.
The Solution
Booking app for outdoor experiences that focuses on critical safety information
Ben Ludolph
Trek
An app for outdoor travelers post pandemic
The application helps it’s users travel outdoors post pandemic. Dedicated to its users safety and supports them in day to day outdoor activities to planned travel by providing essential information about safety restrictions, location health, and mask mandates.
